Welcome

Welcome. My name is Shardul Joshi and I am currently a senior at Andover High School in Andover, MA. I enjoy computer science and hope to pursue a career in which I can utilize that enthusiasm. I am also interested in an entreprenurial career, and feel that pairing computer science and business in college can help build a careet I will enjoy. In addition to being a member of the computer science club at my school, I take part in the school's science team, cross country team (of which I am a captain), Young Democrats of America chapter, and Model United Nations. In recent months, I have been learning Java in a class at school. Outside of school, my hobbies include spending time with friends, playing guitar in my band, Spoonful of Sarcasm (SFOS for short), and tutoring students in mathematics as a tutor at the Russian School of Mathematics.

This is my personal website where I have tried to showcase my skills as a web developer as well as some of my other interests. My resume can be found here. Please contact me if you have any questions. Thank you for visiting!

Portfolio

Syntax Solutions

7PlusH

Take a look at konjoianeducationcenter.com.

Amaury Castillo Electric

Keshia's Cleaning Services
